Introduction

Manga Grove is a local offline manga reading application provided by XinNing Zhang (hereinafter referred to as "we" or "us"). We take your privacy protection very seriously. This Privacy Policy aims to explain how we collect, use, and store your relevant information when you use our services. This Privacy Policy is closely related to the Manga Grove services you use. We hope you read it carefully.

Your use or continued use of our services means that you agree to our collection, use, and storage of your relevant information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or related matters, please contact us at ankefeedback@gmail.com.

1. Information We Collect

Manga Grove is a completely local offline application. We only collect and store the following information to provide basic functionality:

  • Application Log Information: To diagnose issues and improve application performance, we store application runtime logs locally on your device, including error information and performance data. These logs are stored only on your device and are never uploaded to any server.
  • Reading Preferences: Including your reading progress, display settings, language preferences, etc. This information is only used to provide a personalized reading experience on your device.
  • Book Metadata: Basic information about the manga books you import (such as titles, covers, etc.), stored only locally on your device.

Important Note: We do not collect your personal identification information (such as name, email address, phone number, etc.), nor do we use any network tracking technologies (such as cookies, identifiers, etc.) to collect your browsing information.

2. Information Storage

2.1 Methods and Duration of Information Storage
  • All information is stored only locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms provided by the system (such as app sandbox, databases, etc.).
  • We do not upload any information to servers or cloud storage.
  • You can delete all locally stored information at any time by deleting the application.
2.2 Location of Information Storage
  • All information is stored only on your device and is never transferred across borders or stored in the cloud.
  • We do not access, read, or transmit any information from your device.
2.3 Notice When Products or Services Cease Operation
  • When our products or services cease operation, we will notify you through in-app notifications, announcements, and other forms. Since all data is stored only locally on your device, you can decide whether to keep or delete this data.

3. Information Security

We use secure storage mechanisms provided by the system to protect information stored on your device. All data is stored within the app sandbox, and other applications cannot access it. We do not transmit any of your information over the network, so there are no security risks during network transmission.

However, please understand that since all data is stored locally on your device, you need to properly safeguard your device to avoid the risk of information disclosure due to device loss or theft.

4. How We Use Information

We only use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide basic reading functionality on your device;
  • To save your reading progress and preferences so you can continue reading the next time you use the app;
  • To diagnose application issues and improve application performance and stability (using only local log information).

We do not use your information for the following purposes:

  • We do not use it for advertising or marketing promotion;
  • We do not use it for user profiling analysis;
  • We do not use it for any commercial purposes;
  • We do not share or sell your information with third parties.

5. Information Sharing

We do not share, transfer, or disclose your personal information with any third parties. Since all data is stored only locally on your device, we cannot access your data, so there is no possibility of sharing data with third parties.

We do not share any of your information with any third parties, including advertisers, data analytics companies, cloud service providers, etc.

We do not publicly disclose collected personal information. Since we do not collect personal identification information and all data is stored only locally on your device, there is no situation of public disclosure.

According to relevant laws, regulations, and national standards, we may provide information to relevant agencies in accordance with the law in the following circumstances. However, please note that since all data is stored only locally on your device, we cannot directly access this data:

  • Directly related to national security and national defense security;
  • Directly related to public safety, public health, and major public interests;
  • Directly related to criminal investigation, prosecution, trial, and judgment execution.

6. Your Rights

Since all data is stored only locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • You can view, modify, or delete all information stored in the application at any time;
  • You can delete all locally stored data by deleting the application;
  • You can stop using our services at any time.

If you have any questions or suggestions about our privacy protection measures, you can contact us through the contact information provided in this Privacy Policy, and we will handle your feedback promptly.
