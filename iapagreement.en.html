1. Service Description

1.1 Membership Service Definition

The "Manga Grove" Premium membership service is an auto-renewable subscription service that provides you with advanced features of the application. This application offers three subscription period options:

  • Premium (Monthly): Monthly subscription with automatic monthly renewal
  • Premium (Quarterly): Quarterly subscription with automatic renewal every three months
  • Premium (Annual): Annual subscription with automatic yearly renewal

1.2 Payment Authorization

By activating this service, you authorize the application developer (hereinafter referred to as "Developer") to automatically deduct the next billing cycle's membership fee from your accounts, including but not limited to your recharge account, third-party payment accounts linked to your membership account, bank cards, communication accounts, and iTunes account (hereinafter collectively referred to as "Accounts"), when your "Manga Grove" membership service is about to expire.

1.3 Membership Features

Manga Grove is an application that helps you read local manga files on iPhone, iPad, and Mac. The membership service includes the following features:

Free Version Features:

  • Bookshelf Management: Add unlimited books to organize your entire collection
  • Image Format Support: Directly read common image formats like JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP
  • File Import: Import books from local storage, iCloud, NAS, or other cloud services (like Dropbox, OneDrive) through the iOS "Files" app
  • Wi-Fi Transfer: Transfer books from your computer to your device via wireless network
  • Book Tags: Add tags to each book for easy categorization
  • Reading Progress: Automatically record your last reading position for convenient continuation

Premium Membership Exclusive Features:

  • Batch Book Import: Select a folder to import all books at once
  • Batch Wi-Fi Transfer: Transfer multiple books to your device simultaneously
  • One-Click Bookshelf Cleanup: Quickly clear bookshelf contents for easy reorganization

3. Subscription Management

3.1 Subscription Options

"Manga Grove" Premium offers three subscription options:

  • Premium (Monthly): Billed monthly with automatic monthly renewal
  • Premium (Quarterly): Billed quarterly with automatic renewal every three months
  • Premium (Annual): Billed annually with automatic yearly renewal

Specific prices are subject to what is displayed in the App Store.

3.2 Auto-Renewal

Subscriptions will automatically renew at the end of each billing period unless you cancel at least 24 hours before the end of the current subscription period. The system will automatically deduct the fee for the next billing period from your account.

3.3 Canceling Subscription

If you choose to cancel the subscription service, you need to cancel at least 24 hours before your membership expires; otherwise, the system will still automatically renew for the next billing cycle.

3.4 Cancellation Method

For users with Apple in-app purchases (IAP) auto-renewal, the cancellation method is as follows:

  • Open "Settings" on your Apple device
  • Navigate to "iTunes Store & App Store"
  • Tap "Apple ID" and select "View Apple ID"
  • Go to "Account Settings" page and tap "Subscriptions"
  • Cancel the "Manga Grove Membership Service" subscription
